EngBot

I am the designer for EngBot, an AI-based English learning app that tailors its content to suit the user's specific occupation. My role involves creating UI/UX designs in Figma, where I develop user-friendly dashboards, login pages, and personalized questionnaires. Starting from scratch, I ensure that each element maintains a consistent and engaging theme, enhancing the user experience. By focusing on the needs and preferences of different occupations, the app provides a customized learning journey that makes learning English more relevant and effective for users.

Code with Ducks

As the designer for Code with Ducks, an application that teaches programming to young students instructed by Stevens students, I have created interactive and visually appealing designs from scratch to attract more users. This ongoing project is part of our nonprofit organization dedicated to delivering comprehensive, no-cost lessons in AI and Machine Learning to high school students. Our mission is to provide equitable access to high-quality education in these cutting-edge fields, empowering the next generation of learners with essential knowledge.
